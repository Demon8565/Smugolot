Smuglantis Levels — Rules

How the weekly ladder & Smug Points work

The Basics

Every Monday 00:00 UTC, the level board resets. You climb tiers that week through a mix of holding $SMG and community participation. Your Smug Points balance never resets — only the weekly race does.

Weekly Reward

Whoever reaches the highest tier by week's end earns a bonus batch of Smug Points on top of what they've already collected.

Redeem Your Points

  • Tadpole: Telegram badge, holders-only channel, community shoutouts
  • Axolotl: + Early access to announcements, sticker/emoji pack, votes on community decisions
  • Smuglantean: + Merch/NFT whitelist priority, roadmap input, community spotlight
  • Elder: + 1:1 AMA access, custom mascot art, top staking APY once staking launches

Fair Play

Smug Points are a loyalty currency, not $SMG and not a financial instrument. Fake wallets, wash referrals, and snapshot-timing abuse will be excluded from rewards. Hold $SMG to unlock community perks — that's it.
